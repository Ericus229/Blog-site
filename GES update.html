GES Vacancy Announcement: Teaching Positions Open for Qualified Graduates

Date Posted: April 10, 2026

Management of the Ghana Education Service (GES) invites passionate and dedicated graduates with a Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) degree and/or related certificate in Education to apply for teaching positions in the Service.

Job Description

Selected applicants are expected to:

  • Facilitate learning using 21st century technology.
  • Cater for learners with different learning needs and help them achieve excellent learning outcomes.
  • Continuously improve learners' ability to research using new technologies.
  • Use a variety of assessment tools and tasks to accurately assess levels of learner competencies.
  • Use the National Teaching Standards and Code of Conduct to guide practice at all times to ensure professionalism and ethical behaviour.
  • Participate in relevant continuous professional development sessions to stay current.

Eligibility Criteria

Applicants must:

  • Hold a Bachelor's Degree in Education or a relevant Bachelor's Degree with Postgraduate Diploma in Education (PGDE)/Post Graduate Certificate in Education (PGCE), or Diploma in Basic Education.
  • Have completed National Service.
  • Be in good standing with the National Teaching Council (NTC) and possess a valid Teaching License.
  • Demonstrate strong communication and classroom management skills.
  • Be ready and willing to accept posting to deprived districts.

How to Apply

Interested applicants should follow these steps:

  1. Visit the official GES recruitment portal: https://bit.ly/GESteacherrecruitment2026
  2. Complete the online application form.
  3. Upload required documents: Degree/Diploma certificates, National Service Certificate, NTC License, Ghana Card, passport-sized photograph, active email, and phone number. (NB: Documents can be uploaded in any picture format and PDF.)

Application window: April 10 to April 17, 2026.

Signed:
DANIEL FENYI
HEAD OF PUBLIC RELATIONS, GES
