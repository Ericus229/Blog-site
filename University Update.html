UCC Admissions Portal Open for 2026/2027 Undergraduate Applications

Date Posted: April 9, 2026

The University of Cape Coast Admissions Portal is now open to receive applications for the regular undergraduate programme for the 2026/2027 Academic Year.

Candidates who will be writing the WASSCE in 2026 (awaiting results), as well as those who already possess WASSCE results, are eligible to apply.

Cost of E-Voucher

GHc220.00

Where to Buy the E-Voucher

Applicants can purchase the E-Voucher from any of the following outlets:

  • GCB Bank
  • ADB
  • GT Bank
  • Republic Bank
  • Consolidated Bank Ghana (CBG)
  • Zenith Bank
  • Prudential Bank
  • CAL Bank
  • National Investment Bank (NIB)
  • OmniBSIC Bank
  • Fidelity Bank
  • GhanaDotCom

How to Apply

Prospective applicants should visit the official admissions portal to complete their applications online.
