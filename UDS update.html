Applications Are Now Open: UDS 2026/2027 Academic Year Admissions

Date Posted: April 10, 2026

Admission forms for the University for Development Studies (UDS) 2026/2027 academic year are now available for sale. Sale began on Friday, January 16, 2026. Undergraduate forms cost GH₵200 and Postgraduate forms cost GH₵250, purchasable at designated banks. Applications are submitted online, and WASSCE 2026 candidates can apply as awaiting results.

Application Details

  • Application Portal: UDS Admissions Portal
  • Availability: Sale began on January 16, 2026.
  • Eligibility: WASSCE 2026 candidates (awaiting results) are eligible.
  • Application Fees: Undergraduate GH₵200; Postgraduate GH₵250.
  • Deadline (Undergraduate): June 30, 2026.
  • Deadline (Postgraduate): May 31, 2026.
  • Key Requirement: MANDATORY to upload a passport-size photo with a white background.

How to Apply

  1. Purchase E-Voucher: Buy from any designated bank — GCB, Stanbic Bank, ADB, NIB, Prudential Bank, Zenith Bank, and Ecobank.
  2. Create Account: Visit admissions.uds.edu.gh and sign up using the PIN and Serial Number from your e-voucher.
  3. Fill Application: Complete all required fields and upload a passport-size photograph with a white background.
  4. Submit: Submit your completed application online before the deadline. The deadline for postgraduate applications is May 31, 2026; undergraduate applications close June 30, 2026.

Applicants can purchase the e-voucher at all branches of the listed banks. It is mandatory to upload a passport-size photograph with a white background before submission. Applicants are encouraged to review the detailed entry requirements for their preferred course before proceeding to the portal.
